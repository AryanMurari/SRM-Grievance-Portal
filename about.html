Grievance Policy for College Students :-

Introduction

This policy outlines the guidelines for filing grievances on this website.

What can be reported?

Academic Concerns:

  • Grading issues
  • Faculty disputes
  • Unfair assessments

Non-Academic Concerns:

  • Discrimination
  • Harassment
  • Bullying
  • Accessibility issues
  • Housing problems
  • Violation of college policies

What cannot be reported?

  • Personal disputes between students unrelated to the college environment.
  • Minor inconveniences not affecting student well-being or academic progress.

Steps to file a grievance

  1. Create an account: Register on the website with your college ID and a secure password.
  2. Initiate a complaint: Choose the appropriate category (academic or non-academic) for your grievance.
  3. Provide details: Fill out the online form with a clear and concise description of the issue.
    • Include relevant details like dates, names of individuals involved (if applicable), and any supporting evidence (documents, witness statements).
  4. Submit the grievance: Once you've reviewed the information for accuracy, submit the form electronically.
  5. Acknowledge receipt: You will receive an automated email acknowledging your grievance and a reference number for future communication.

Confidentiality

All grievances will be treated with confidentiality. You have the right to request anonymity during the initial stages of the process.

Response Timeline

A designated college representative will review your grievance within [Number] business days. You will receive updates on the investigation process via email. The representative will aim to reach a resolution within [Number] business days, following college procedures.

Additional Information

  • This website serves as a platform for initial reporting.
  • Depending on the nature of the grievance, the college might require further documentation or in-person meetings.
  • Students are encouraged to explore informal resolution options with faculty advisors, department heads, or relevant college personnel before filing a formal grievance.

By using this website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this grievance policy.
