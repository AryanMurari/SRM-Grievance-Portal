SRM IST Grievance Portal

The SRM Institute of Science and Technology Grievance Portal is a comprehensive platform designed to address concerns, feedback, and grievances from all departments within the institution. At SRM IST, we recognize the importance of fostering an environment where students, faculty, and staff feel heard, supported, and empowered to voice their concerns. With this understanding, the Grievance Portal serves as a centralized hub for managing and resolving issues promptly and effectively. One of the key features of the Grievance Portal is its inclusivity, ensuring that all departments within the institution are covered. Whether it pertains to academic matters, administrative procedures, campus facilities, or any other aspect of campus life, individuals from any department can utilize the portal to express their grievances. The portal operates on a user-friendly interface, allowing individuals to submit their concerns easily while providing necessary details for efficient resolution. Upon submission, grievances are promptly routed to the respective department heads or designated authorities for review and action. This streamlined process ensures that grievances are addressed in a timely manner, promoting transparency and accountability throughout the institution. Moreover, the Grievance Portal also facilitates communication between stakeholders, enabling ongoing dialogue and follow-up on the status of reported issues. Through regular updates and notifications, individuals can track the progress of their grievances, fostering trust and confidence in the institution's commitment to addressing concerns promptly. By encompassing all departments within the institution, the SRM Institute of Science and Technology Grievance Portal promotes a culture of accountability, transparency, and continuous improvement. It serves as a testament to our dedication to fostering a supportive and inclusive campus environment where every voice is valued and every concern is addressed with diligence and care.
